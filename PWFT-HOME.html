This is a digital cookbook dedicated to the golden trio of breakfast foods; pancakes, waffles and french toast.

Each page will explore a recipe that is quick to make, simple to follow and delicious every time.

The internet should make cooking easier, yet online cooking resources can be difficult to navigate, filled with advertisments and pay walls, and designed without any attention to user experience. This website is designed to change all of that, and provide a free resource that anyone can access, share and refer to.

Thank you for visiting and enjoy!
