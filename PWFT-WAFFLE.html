1 cup of flour
1/2 tbsp of white sugar
2 tsp of baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp of cinnamon
1 egg
3/4 cup of milk
1/4 cup of oil
1/2 tsp of vanilla

single batch : serves two

preperation

1x waffle iron
1x large bowl
1x whisk

set waffle iron to desired heat and keep hot until ready





step one

combine all dry ingredients in bowl

1 cup flour
1/2 tbsp sugar
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp cinnamon




step two

make a well in the center and beat an egg in the bowl, then add:

3/4 cup milk
1/4 cup oil
1/2 tsp vanilla

whisk until smooth



step three

pour around 1/4 cup of batter in the heated waffle iron

close the lid and wait for the waffle to rise, and the steam to dissipate




step four

when the waffle is crisp and golden brown, open the lid and gently place waffle on a wire rack

repeat process and use up all the batter




final step

plate waffles and serve with butter and maple syrup
